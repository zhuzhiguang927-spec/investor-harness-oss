## 一、行业概览

Grid storage connects renewable generation, transmission stability, and demand-side flexibility. The sector is moving from pilot projects to repeat utility procurement cycles. `市场共识`

## 二、行业增长分析

Growth is driven by renewable penetration, peak-load management, and grid-stability requirements. The main uncertainty is whether project economics remain attractive when interest rates and battery prices move against developers.

## 四、需求侧驱动

Demand is strongest where renewable curtailment, grid congestion, and peak pricing overlap. Utility procurement is more durable than subsidy-driven residential demand.

## 五、供给侧变化

Battery supply is becoming less constrained, while inverter certification and grid-connection expertise remain bottlenecks.

## 六、价格与利润传导

Battery cell price declines help integrators first, then pass through to utilities. PCS suppliers benefit if volume growth offsets pricing pressure.
